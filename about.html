Scott Simon

I have been taking photos since the early 80's!

I started taking pictures with a camera my grandfather gave me as a present for my 5th birthday. It was one of the best gifts I have ever received: a gift that keeps on giving!

I have lived in Chicago my entire life so many of the images you will see depict Chicago in all it's greatness.

My favorite kind of picture is developed the old fashioned way: Silver print using black and white film; There is no other experience like it!
